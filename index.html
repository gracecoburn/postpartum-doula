Welcome to Grace Coburn's Postpartum Doula Services

The Greek word doula means servant, my mission as a postpartum doula is to lovingly serve you and your new family through this transformative time. I am a postpartum doula and not a birth doula, she is involved in the labor of mother and baby. The postpartum doula comes at a scheduled time to aid the family through the postpartum phase (after the baby has arrived). Doulas use the acronym N.E.A.R. to describe what we aim to do. Nurture, Educate, Assess and Refer. I will Nurture you, the primary client, and family as a whole, through giving emotional support to helping prepare nutritional meals within the home. I also offer light household help, changing out and folding laundry, keeping the kitchen tidy and dishes moving. An often underestimated aspect of adding a new member to the family is the sibling and spousal dynamic needing to evolve. I love to help support families with existing siblings and promote bonding between the whole pack. I Educate, not always through direct instruction but often through discovery and encouragement. Having knowledge of how a typical recovery after birth and newborn care goes helps me support you as you navigate this new space. When something catches my eye I Assess, not to be confused with diagnosing someone. I am not a medical professional. However, when things I have assessed to be outside of my knowledge or scope I Refer you to a trusted source to connect you with more information, services or resources than I can provide. I am N.E.A.R. In addition to what most postpartum doulas offer I love to share my knowledge and experience with cloth diapering and elimination communication, also known as infant potty training (an alternative to diapering practiced in most of Asia and Africa). With the experience I have under my belt I can offer support if the family is already planning to do these things and offer suggestions on how to create systems to be successful in these endeavors. Every mother and family can benefit from the TLC I have to offer, please pop on over to my about me and contact page to reach out and see if we would be a good match!
